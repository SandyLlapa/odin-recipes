Fried Beef Tacos

These delectable crispy fried beef tacos, also known as "Arizona Tacos," are currently gaining popularity on TikTok. The preparation involves pressing raw meat onto a tortilla and then frying it until it achieves a perfect, crunchy exterior while retaining a tender and juicy filling. The best part is that these tacos are relatively simple to make. Let me walk you through the process:

Ingredients

  • 8 (5-inch) corn tortillas
  • 1 pound ground sirloin
  • 2 tablespoons taco seasoning
  • 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt, divided
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil, or as needed for frying
  • 1/2 cup minced white onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • lime wedges, for serving
  • 1/4 cup salsa, or as needed, for serving
